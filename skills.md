# AI Skills utilized in the Matchmaker

## 1. Visual Recognition
- Processing multimodal inputs (images) to extract structured food metadata.
- Distinguishing between cooked meals and raw ingredients.

## 2. Geospatial Optimization
- Calculating Haversine distances to ensure minimal transport time for perishables.
- Dynamic placement of NGOs for demo-local matching.

## 3. Dynamic Scoring
- Multi-factor algorithmic ranking based on food-NGO compatibility.
- Real-time adjustment of scores based on time of day (late-night logic).
